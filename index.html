About Me

Computer Science student, Content Writer, Graphic Designer

Hey! I’m Apoorv Sharma, and I’m a sophomore studying Computer Science at Birla Institute Of Technology, Mesra located in the Land of Forests, Jharkhand. From writing four-lined programs in C to making my own webpage, my love for programming has increased ever so much. I’m a problem-solver. Be it finding the most optimized solution or presenting something in the best way I can, I like to think my way around.

Born and brought up in Kanpur city, I moved to Kota for my high school years, with a dream to get into a premiere engineering college. I secured 98.99 percentile in JEE Mains and got selected in BIT Mesra for B.TECH in Computer Science. I started my programming journey with CS50, a computer science introductory course from Harvard which gave me the start I needed. From doing online courses and reading articles on the internet, to solving problems on Codeforces, I’m always trying to learn new things.
